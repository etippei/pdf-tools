How to Compress PDF Files Online

Reduce PDF file size while maintaining quality. Perfect for email attachments, faster uploads, and saving storage space.

  1. Upload your PDF file
  2. Choose compression level: Low, Medium, or High
  3. Click "Compress PDF"
  4. Download your compressed PDF file

Compression Levels

  • Low - Maximum compression (30-40% of original size) - Best for email
  • Medium - Balanced compression (50-60% of original size) - Recommended
  • High - Minimum compression (70-80% of original size) - Best quality

Common Use Cases

  • Email attachments - Reduce file size to meet email limits
  • Website uploads - Reduce file size for faster uploads
  • Storage optimization - Save space on your device
  • Faster sharing - Send smaller files to colleagues

Frequently Asked Questions

Is compressing PDFs free?

Yes! Free users can compress up to 5 PDFs per day. Create a free account to track your usage, or upgrade to Pro for unlimited compression.

How much can I reduce the file size?

Typically 50-80% reduction depending on the content. Low compression gives the smallest files, High compression preserves the best quality.

Does compression affect quality?

Higher compression levels may slightly reduce image quality. For text-heavy documents, the impact is minimal.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.
