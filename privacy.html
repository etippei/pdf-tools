Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. No Data Collection

PDFHub.AI processes all PDF files locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. We do not store, analyze, or share any of your documents.

2. Local Processing Only

All PDF operations (merge, split, compress, convert) and AI features happen directly on your device or through secure API calls. Your files never leave your computer.

3. AI Processing

For AI features (chat, summary), only extracted text is sent to our AI service provider. We do not store your documents or chat history.

4. Account Data

When you create an account, we store your email address and encrypted password. This data is used solely for authentication and Pro status verification.

5. Cookies

We use essential cookies for login functionality. Third-party services (Gumroad, Google AdSense) may set their own cookies for payment processing and advertising.

6. Contact

Questions? Contact us at etiplpl@outlook.com

Your Privacy Matters

We built PDFHub.AI with privacy-first principles. No upload, no tracking, no storage.
