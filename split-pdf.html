How to Split PDF Pages Online

Extract specific pages from a PDF file. Extract a page range or specific pages by number. Perfect for sharing only the pages you need.

  1. Upload your PDF file
  2. Choose "Extract page range" or "Extract single pages"
  3. Enter the page numbers or range
  4. Click "Extract Pages"
  5. Download the new PDF with only the pages you selected

Common Use Cases

  • Extract specific pages - Get only the pages you need from a document
  • Remove unwanted pages - Extract all pages except the ones you don't want
  • Create separate documents - Split a large PDF into smaller files
  • Share limited content - Share only relevant pages with others
