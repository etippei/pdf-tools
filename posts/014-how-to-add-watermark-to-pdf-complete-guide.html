How to Add Watermark to PDF - Complete Guide

Watermarks protect your documents from unauthorized use and reinforce your brand identity. Whether you need to mark documents as "Confidential," add your company logo, or prevent content theft, adding watermarks is essential for professional document management.

Why Add Watermarks to PDF?

  • Copyright protection - Deters unauthorized copying and distribution
  • Brand visibility - Promotes your company on every page
  • Document status - Mark as "Draft," "Confidential," or "Final"
  • Professional identity - Reinforces your brand with logos and text
  • Prevent misuse - Discourages screenshot theft

Pro Tips for Best Results

  • Use subtle opacity - 15-30% opacity provides protection without distracting from content
  • Choose contrasting colors - Make sure watermark is visible on both light and dark pages
  • Consider the purpose - "Confidential" should be prominent; branding should be subtle
  • Test one page - Preview on a single page before applying to all pages
  • Use diagonal placement - Covers more area and is harder to crop out

Common Use Cases
