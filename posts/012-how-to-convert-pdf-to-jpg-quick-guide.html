How to Convert PDF to JPG - Quick Guide

Need to share a PDF as images? Converting PDF to JPG lets you extract pages as individual pictures, perfect for presentations, social media, or previews. This guide shows you how in seconds.

Why Convert PDF to JPG?

  • Easy sharing - JPGs display instantly on any device without needing PDF readers
  • Social media ready - Upload JPG images directly to Instagram, Facebook, or LinkedIn
  • Preview pages - Show thumbnails of your document before sharing the full file
  • Website friendly - Use JPG images on websites and blogs

Frequently Asked Questions

Is converting PDF to JPG really free? Yes! Free users get 5 conversions per day. Pro users get unlimited conversions and higher quality options.

Does the image quality drop? Only if you choose Low quality. Use High quality or 300 DPI for full resolution.

Do you store my PDF files? Never. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

Can I convert scanned PDFs? Yes. The tool works with both digital and scanned PDF documents.

What's the difference between JPG and PNG? JPG is smaller with slightly lower quality. PNG is larger with lossless quality. Use JPG for sharing, PNG for archiving.

Can I convert specific pages only? Yes. You can select a page range (e.g., pages 3-7) instead of converting all pages.
