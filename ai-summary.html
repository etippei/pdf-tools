How to Generate AI Summaries from PDF

Get a concise, intelligent summary of any PDF document in seconds. Choose from short, medium, or long summaries based on your needs.

  1. Upload your PDF file (max 10MB for free users, 20MB for Pro users)
  2. Choose your preferred summary length: Short, Medium, or Long
  3. Click "Generate Summary"
  4. Copy, download as TXT, or download as DOCX

Summary Length Options

  • Short (3-5 sentences) - Quick overview for fast reading
  • Medium (8-10 sentences) - Balanced summary with key details (Recommended)
  • Long (15-20 sentences) - Comprehensive summary for in-depth understanding

Common Use Cases

  • Executive summaries - Get the key points from business reports
  • Academic research - Quickly understand the main findings of papers
  • News articles - Get the gist without reading the full article
  • Legal documents - Understand contracts and agreements faster
  • Book summaries - Get the main ideas from non-fiction books
