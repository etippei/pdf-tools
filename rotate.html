How to Rotate PDF Pages

Sometimes PDF pages are scanned or created in the wrong orientation. Rotating PDF pages allows you to fix page orientation.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Upload your PDF file
  2. Choose to rotate all pages or specific pages
  3. Select rotation angle (90°, 180°, or 270°)
  4. Click "Rotate PDF" button
  5. Download your rotated file
