Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

At AIPDFLY, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Information: If you create an account, we store your email address and encrypted password.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymized usage statistics to improve our tools (e.g., number of files processed).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for authentication and session management.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our PDF tools and services
  • To authenticate your account and manage your Pro subscription
  • To improve and optimize our website performance
  • To send you important updates about our services

3. Data Storage & Security

All PDF processing happens locally in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to our servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your account information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare: For CDN and security
  • Google Analytics: For anonymized usage statistics
  • Gumroad: For subscription payments
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant ads

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt-out of non-essential cookies
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: etiplpl@outlook.com
